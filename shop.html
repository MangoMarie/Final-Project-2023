When it comes to buying tea and tea accessories, it can be pretty exciting. Please email us at TeaTyme@gmail.com to set up a free tea consultation and to order loose tea.

Tea accessories can range from an assortment of teapots, teacups, and tea infusers, to tea trays, electric kettles, and tea warmers. When you start shopping, it's important to consider what type of tea accessories you need. If you're just starting out with tea, it's best to start with the basics like a teapot, a few cups, and an infuser or strainer. If you prefer loose leaf tea, you'll likely need a teapot with an infuser or basket, and a few tea cups to enjoy your tea in.
If you're looking for something a bit more sophisticated, you may want to consider purchasing a tea chest, tea tray, or an electric kettle. Tea chests are ideal for storing your favourite tea blends, while tea trays help to organize all of your tea accessories.
Electric kettles are great for quickly boiling water for your tea, and tea warmers help keep your tea warm for an extended period of time. Once you have narrowed down what type of tea accessories you want, email us and we will provide you some of our favorite varieties to pick from. With your first purchase with us, we will also include a sample tea bag of our seasons favorite tea flavor combinations!
