Radical self care is an approach to taking care of yourself and your well-being with intention, purpose, and commitment. It’s an ongoing practice that involves setting boundaries, self-love, and self-care. It’s a practice that can be a tool for social justice and personal transformation. Radical self-care is about getting to the root causes of our suffering, understanding our inner worlds, and healing our relationships with ourselves and the world around us. It’s about creating space to reflect, to explore, and to nurture ourselves. It’s about recognizing our needs, our desires, and our limitations, and honoring them. It’s about being honest with ourselves and our feelings, and challenging ourselves to grow and learn.
It’s about taking time to nourish and nurture ourselves in whatever ways feel right for us. It’s about understanding and accepting that we are always changing and growing. It’s about recognizing our strengths and our weaknesses, our successes and our failures. It’s about learning to trust our instincts, our intuition, and our decisions. It’s about loving and respecting ourselves unconditionally, even when we make mistakes. It’s about accepting and embracing all of who we are. Radical self-care is a powerful tool for personal growth and transformation. It’s an ongoing practice of self-love, self-care, and self-awareness that can help us to live our best lives.
Please remember to have healthy boundaries, with youself, and others.
You can not pour from and empty cup. Replenish yourself.
