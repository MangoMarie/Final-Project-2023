Sweet, Salty, Crunchy, Oh My...


When considering what to eat with tea, it is important to recognize your own personal tastes and preferences. There are numerous things that can be served with tea, ranging from savory to sweet, and there are many different types of tea that can be enjoyed.

Some popular savory choices to enjoy with tea include cheese and crackers, toast with butter or jam, sandwiches, soup, and tea eggs. For those with a sweet tooth, pastries, cookies, cakes, scones, and tarts can serve as the perfect accompaniment.

Whether it is a classic afternoon tea or a more modern tea experience, certain foods can help elevate the tea drinking experience. For example, if you are a fan of English Breakfast tea, enjoying the tea with traditionally British foods such as scones, clotted cream, and jam can be a delightful way to indulge in the morning or afternoon.

If you are a lover of green tea, combining it with fresh fruits and light nibbles such as sushi or eggrolls can be a great way to enjoy the tea. Of course, tea can also be enjoyed plain.
